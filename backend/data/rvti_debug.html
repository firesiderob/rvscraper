Find a Certified Technician

Look for the RVTI Certification

RVs come in various styles, from motorhomes to travel trailers, each with its own unique systems and components. When your RV needs service or repairs, its important to find a qualified technician who can ensure your vehicle is in good hands.

One of the most important factors to consider when hiring an RV technician is their certification. The RV Technical Institute offers a nationally recognized certification for RV technicians, with a curriculum built in tandem with the manufacturers of RVs and their component parts, ensuring they have the necessary skills and knowledge. Technicians who have completed the program have demonstrated their expertise in RV systems, maintenance, diagnostics, and repair.

Whether you're experiencing a minor issue or in need of comprehensive service, the RV Technical Institutes network of trusted professionals ensures your RV is in expert hands, giving you peace of mind to enjoy your adventures.
